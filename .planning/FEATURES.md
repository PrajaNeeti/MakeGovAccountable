# PrajaNeeti Features & 3-Pillar UX Architecture

This repository contains the source code for **PrajaNeeti**, an open-source civic accountability platform built around **3 Core UX Pillars**:

---

## 🏛️ Pillar 1: Philosophy & Vision (`/vision`, `/milestones`)
*“Quiet accountability over political noise.”*

- **Governance Essays**: Dedicated articles explaining what proper governance means and moving conversations from political point-scoring to honest record-keeping.
- **Viksit Bharat & Developed Nation Principles**: Frameworks detailing why institutional transparency, fiscal discipline, and civic duty (Bhagavad Gita & Chanakya Neeti principles) are required to build a developed state.
- **The PrajaNeeti Manifesto**: Open-source manifesto inviting citizens, developers, and researchers to contribute.
- **Platform & Data Milestones Ledger (`/milestones`)**: Interactive timeline showcasing platform engineering milestones and custom data acquisition progress.

---

## 💬 Pillar 2: Civic Discourse & Concerns (`/forums`, `/concerns`, `/submit`)
*“Elevating public discussion & consolidating civic willpower.”*

- **Letters & Discourse Forum (`/forums`)**: Editorial-style discussion forum for serious, non-partisan civic dialogue.
- **Civic Concerns Ledger (`/concerns`)**: Searchable index of public grievances grouped by location, state, and department.
- **Raise a Concern Portal (`/submit`)**: Simple citizen submission portal with **AI Semantic De-duplication** to match new issues against existing records and prevent duplicate entries.
- **1-Click Navbar Action**: Prominent `+ Raise Concern` CTA button accessible on every page.

---

## 📊 Pillar 3: Multi-Branch Data & Responsible Official Accountability (`/transparency`, `/mplads`, `/politicians`, `/dashboards`)
*“Audited public ledgers & person-by-person responsibility.”*

- **Candidate Affidavits & Legal Records (MyNeta / ADR)**: Financial disclosures (declared total assets, liabilities, net worth), educational qualifications, and declared criminal IPC case warning badges per MP (`/politicians/[id]`).
- **PRS Legislative Track Record**: MP parliamentary activity indicators—attendance percentage, questions asked, debates participated, and private member bills introduced (`/politicians/[id]`).
- **Cabinet Allocation of Business (AoB) Rules**: Statutory Second Schedule executive subject-matter mandates per Union Ministry/Department (`/dashboards`).
- **DoPT IAS Civil Servant Roster**: Senior civil servant postings, batch allotment years, cadres, pay levels, and qualifications linked to departments (`/dashboards`).
- **State MLA Local Area Development (MLALAD)**: State assembly constituency allocations, expenditure, unspent balances, and completed works count (Gujarat Pilot) (`/mplads`).
- **National Judicial Data Grid (NJDG) Aggregates**: High Court and Subordinate Court case pendency, disposed cases, civil/criminal splits, and 10+ year delayed case backlogs (`/dashboards`).
- **MPLADS Fund Oversight Ledger (`/mplads`)**: Full-depth audit of ₹11,538+ Crores in Member of Parliament Local Area Development Scheme funds across 36 Indian States & UTs.
- **Itemized Ground Works Ledger**: Granular index of verified local projects (water pipelines, road construction, school repairs) displaying exact cost in ₹, location, and MP attribution.
- **Open Data Export Center**: Instant CSV & JSON dataset downloads for research and developer integration.
